How to make a tea

Steps to make a tea

  1. First take a bowl
  2. Put on stove
  3. Light up stove
  4. Put water in bowl
  5. Put sugar
  6. Put tea leaf
  7. Put ginger (Optional)
  8. Wait 5 min
  9. Put milk
  10. Wait 2 min
  11. Now tea is ready
